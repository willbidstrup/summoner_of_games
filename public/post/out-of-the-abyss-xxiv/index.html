DM/Designer Notes

We had a climactic battle today and were lucky enough to be semi-prepared. The spell magic circle evened the odds a bit, in what was a deadly encounter with many undead, included my favourites, banshees, and an undead mind flayer. The various wails and gaze attacks had my character on the floor multiple times. Lucky magic circle doesn’t take concentration.

We had an interesting example of being first not being the best when our rogue was turned to stone by a medusa. Thinking about it now, I wonder if he should have acted first and then been turned to stone on the medusa’s turn. I won’t bring that up anytime soon… Because of this, we had our first PC death today. Well, it was more of a PC petrification. It was scary and impactful.
